About 4D

4D is an immensely popular game in both Malaysia and Singapore. In Malaysia, Magnum 4D holds the distinction of being the first legalized 4D operator licensed by the Malaysian Government. Subsequently, other lottery operators such as Da Ma Cai and Sports Toto have followed suit.

Da Ma Cai is managed by Pan Malaysian Pools Sdn. Bhd, which also oversees racing bets, while the other two operate as independent companies. Singapore Pools, on the other hand, is the exclusive provider of gambling games in Singapore.

The 4D game is available every Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday in both countries. Occasionally, there may be a Special Draw held on Tuesdays in Malaysia.

Singapore Pools 4D

To play 4D, pick a number from 0000 to 9999. It costs at least $1, including tax. Draws happen every Wednesday, Saturday, and Sunday at 6:30 pm. There are 23 winning numbers in five prize categories each draw. If you bet on any of these 23 numbers, you win a prize.

Singapore Pools Toto

To play TOTO, choose at least six numbers from 1 to 49. It costs at least $1, including tax. Draws happen every Monday and Thursday at 6:30 pm. If it's a cascade draw, it's at 9:30 pm. Each draw selects six Winning Numbers and one Additional Number. If your chosen numbers match at least three of the Winning Numbers, you win a prize.
